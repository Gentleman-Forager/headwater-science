Ingredient Integrity

Every ingredient in Headwater has a purpose — for flavor, function, or daily usability. Nothing extra, nothing artificial, nothing filler.

L-Theanine — Supports Calm, Focused Mental Energy

L-Theanine is an amino acid naturally found in green tea. It supports increased alpha-wave activity in the brain — the pattern associated with calm, steady focus. This helps you think clearly without feeling wired or overstimulated.

Vitamin C — Supports Cellular Defense and Pairs With Chaga

Vitamin C acts as an antioxidant and also works synergistically with Chaga’s natural compounds. Together they help stabilize free radicals, support recovery from daily stress, and reinforce cellular resilience.

Natural Fruit Essences — Clean Flavor Without Sweet Overload

These essences create Headwater’s crisp, refreshing taste. They keep the flavor bright without heaviness or syrupy sweetness, making the drink enjoyable as a daily ritual.

Light Organic Agave — Smooth Sweetness, No Aftertaste

A small amount of organic agave rounds out the flavor profile. With only 5g of sugar per can, it supports a smooth finish without artificial sweeteners or sugar alcohols.

No Mushroom Flavor — Just Clean, Refreshing Taste

Proper extraction isolates functional compounds from Lion’s Mane and Chaga without carrying over earthy or woody flavors. That’s why Headwater tastes like a premium sparkling beverage — clean, bright, and refreshing — not like mushrooms.
